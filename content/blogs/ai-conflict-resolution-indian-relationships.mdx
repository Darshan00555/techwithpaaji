## 1. What is AI Conflict Resolution? (Digital Mediator)
AI Conflict Resolution refers to the use of AI tools to facilitate communication, identify emotional triggers, and suggest constructive ways to resolve disagreements. It’s like having a neutral, non-judgmental relationship coach in your pocket 24/7.

According to a study by **[Psychology Today India](https://www.psychologytoday.com/intl/basics/relationships)**, "AI can act as a cooling-off mechanism." When we are angry, our emotions take over. AI helps by using logic and empathy-based algorithms to show us where we went wrong. In the high-stress environments of Mumbai or Gurgaon, this digital sanity check is a lifesaver.

## 2. Using AI to Phrase Difficult Conversations
One of the biggest causes of fights in India is *how* we say things. 
- **Toxic Text Rephrasing:** Instead of sending "You never care about my parents," an AI might suggest, "I feel unheard when we discuss family visits. Can we talk about it?".
- **Passive-Aggressive Decoder:** AI can scan a series of WhatsApp messages and tell you, "Your partner seems overwhelmed, not angry. Maybe give them space?".
- **Conflict Mirroring:** AI can summarize an argument and show both partners what they said, helping them see their own behavior from a distance.
